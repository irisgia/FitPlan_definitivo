MIDDLE BACK

 
 
 

 

The primary muscles in the mid-back are the rhomboids, lower trapezius, and middle trapezius. The rhomboids are responsible for squeezing the shoulder blades together and down towards the thighs. The lower trapezius is responsible for moving the shoulder blades down toward the thighs, and the middle trapezius is responsible for squeezing the shoulder blades together.
